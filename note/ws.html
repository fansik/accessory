What is a Sentence? Quiz(English club)

1. A sentence is a set of words with a subject and a predicate.

2. The subject of a sentence is what it's about.

3. The predicate is what's said about the subject.

4. "My dog likes to chase cats." The predicate is "likes to chases cats".

5. The predicate of a sentence always includes a verb.

6. An Imperative sentence like "Stop!" don't have to include the subject.

7. In the sentence "Wait a minute!", the predicate has three words.

8. A full sentence expresses a complete thought.

9. Which is a full sentence? Come here. "Come here" is a full sentence.

10. Which is not a full sentence? All of them. "All of them" is not a full sentence.
