GOING TO GMAE 2(EnglishClub)

Complete each sentence with a word from the list:

to bug	to fail	to get    to go    to lose    to rain    to retire    to see

to strike    to visit
1. If you eat to much food, you're going to get fat.
2. If she doesn't study more, she's going fail.
3. Look at those dark clouds. It's going to rain.
4. Unless you work harder, you're going to lose your job.
5. If I make a lot of money, I'm going to buy a big house.
6. While we're in India, we're going to visit the Taj Mahal.
7. If the police catch him, he's going to go to jail.
8. Unless their pay is increased, the workers are going to strike.
9. As soon as the movie opens, we're going to see it.
10. When I'm sixty years old, I'm going to retire.
